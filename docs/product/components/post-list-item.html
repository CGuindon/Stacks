{% header "h2", "Examples" %}

Post list items are a flexible component capable of previewing various content types—questions (with or without answers) or articles. Post list items are meant to be flexible, with most of its individual pieces being optional. Let's start with a full example of a few questions and an article interleaved.

{% header "h3", "Full" %}

At their most complete, list items can contain:

  • Title
  • Excerpt
  • Tags & other categorization
  • Author & creation date
  • Stats & various states
  • Contextual menu
  • Activity indicator
