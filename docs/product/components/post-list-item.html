{% header "h2", "Examples" %}

Post list items are a flexible component capable of previewing various content types—questions or articles. Post list items are meant to be flexible, allowing for each individual piece to be removed. Let’s start with a full example of a few questions and an article interleaved.

{% header "h3", "Full" %}

At their most complete, post list items are presented in two columns and can contain:

  • Title
  • Excerpt
  • Tags & other categorization
  • Author & creation date
  • Stats & various states
  • Contextual menu

At the smallest breakpoint, the layout will switch to a single column.
